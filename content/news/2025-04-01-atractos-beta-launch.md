Nebyss Innovation today announced the beta launch of Atractos, its domain intelligence platform designed for organisations operating in high-stakes, knowledge-intensive environments.

Atractos is built around the principle that AI deployed in critical industries must preserve human judgment at the centre of decision-making. The platform provides structured AI pipelines where domain experts remain integral to output validation, traceability, and continuous calibration.

The beta programme is initially focused on the energy and public policy sectors, where the team has the deepest domain expertise. Organisations interested in participating can register through the contact page.

"We built Atractos to solve the problem we kept running into in the field," said the Nebyss team. "Generic AI applied to critical operations without real domain structure creates risk, not value. Atractos is our answer to that."

The platform is available by invitation during the beta period.

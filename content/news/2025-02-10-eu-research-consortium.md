---
title: "Nebyss Joins EU-Funded Research Consortium on Responsible AI"
date: "2025-02-10"
excerpt: "Nebyss Innovation has been selected as an industry partner in a European research programme focused on transparent and accountable AI systems in the public sector."
category: "research"
---

Nebyss Innovation has joined a European research consortium examining the governance and technical foundations of responsible AI deployment in public sector contexts. The programme, supported by EU research funding, brings together academic institutions, public bodies, and industry partners from across the continent.

Nebyss's participation focuses on the intersection of domain specificity and accountability — specifically, how AI systems can be designed so that their decision logic remains interpretable to non-technical stakeholders including regulators, affected communities, and elected officials.

"Accountability in AI isn't just a technical property," the team noted. "It has to extend to the people who are affected by AI-driven decisions, not just the people who build and operate the systems."

Work under the programme is expected to run through the end of 2026, with interim findings to be published open access.
